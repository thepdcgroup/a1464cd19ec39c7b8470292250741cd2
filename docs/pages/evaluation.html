Evaluation
Reddit is a ______ website where users discuss various topics.


social media
The various communities on Reddit are called ______.


subreddits
Individual contributions on Reddit are known as ______.


posts
User responses to posts are written as ______.


comments
Positive feedback on posts is given through ______.


upvotes
Negative feedback on content is expressed via ______.


downvotes
Accumulated points for user activity are known as ______.


karma
The main page displaying popular content is called the ______.


front page
To spread a post to other platforms, users can ______ it.


share
Premium features on Reddit can be accessed with ______ membership.


Reddit Gold
Following a subreddit to see its content on your homepage is called ______.


subscribing
The continuous stream of posts on your homepage is known as the ______.


feed
Inappropriate content can be flagged through ______.


reporting
Direct communication between users is done via a ______.


PM
The area displaying additional subreddit information is called the ______.


sidebar
Posts that gain rapid attention are often labeled as ______.


hot
The sorting option for the most recent content is known as ______.


new
Posts with divisive opinions are sometimes categorized as ______.


controversial
The highest rated posts in a community are featured in the ______ section.


top
If a user wishes to modify their submission, they can use the ______ function.


editing
When a post is no longer needed, a user may choose ______.


removing
A customizable label on a post that indicates its category is called ______.


flair
A feature that allows users to combine several subreddits into one view is known as a ______.


multireddit
An interactive post where users can vote on options is called a ______.


poll
The option to bookmark a post for later is available through the ______ feature.


save
